❭ About 'Untitled in E Minor' (see SoundCloud)

A short piece I wrote in 2019 for the classical guitar and then recorded and uploaded to SoundCloud. I am still unsure about a name for it, so it is 'Untitled' for now. There's little to it so I may come back to it at some point to develop it further (but realistically it will probably get left behind). Here is the guitar tablature for it as it stands:

The tab itself has been copied in from a direct export of the music which was written up in Power Tab Editor, an excellent piece of software that I use extensively to capture and (re-)listen to any significant musical lines I think up (very useful as I would quickly forget them otherwise). It is actually really old on a general software timescale (last stable release 2006!) but it is free and does the job perfectly so I've never felt the need to find a similar modern tool.

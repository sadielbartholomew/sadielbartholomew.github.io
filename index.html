About me

I am a young UK-based professional (Scientific Software Engineer by title), originally from Newcastle upon Tyne. In 2017 I completed an integrated master's degree in physics (MPhys) at Durham University.

My interests:

  • Technology, specifically: scientific computing; creative programming & generative design; data analytics & statistics; data visualisation; innovative/entrepreneurial technological ventures.
  • Visual art: appreciation of, with varied specific interests within both the traditional/fine & conceptual spheres; production of own work.
  • Chess: mainly reading about theory
  • Music: composition of & listening to & playing guitar-based music, either: alternative/post-punk(-revival) rock or classical, particularly Bach lute work transcriptions.
  • Science & mathematics, especially: particle (high-energy) physics; other big-data-driven science, such as bioinformatics & cosmology; quantum field theory & group theory; geometry & topology.
  • Savoury cooking
  • Walking
  • Appreciation of nature & wildlife
  • Football: following at club level as a NUFC supporter; unfortunately no longer playing or refereeing.
